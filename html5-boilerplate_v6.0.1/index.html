Vision: Slow down the risks that greenhouse gases impose on the environment and contribute to the fight against climate change


General Description

Black Gold is a non-profit company that believes in composting to help improve our environment by lowering greenhouse gases. Lowering greenhouse gases will help decrease the intensity of natural climate change and contribute towards the fight against a hotter climate. Black Gold believes that the world shouldnâ€™t have to suffer because of our technological advancement. Advancements like cars, industrial buildings, and use of synthetic fertilizers should move us forward and not have consequences. Our non-profit is barely in its infant stage, however, we know with the right team and the right mindset, then we can make an important impact that will live on through generations.
